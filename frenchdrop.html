The french drop is an amazing coin disapearance. Lets learn how it works.

  • 1. First hold the coin at the tip of your fingers. (Shown in diagram above)
  • 2. Hover your hand over the coin like you're about to grab the coin.
  • 3. Drop the coin into your palm. Then close your top hand where the coin was.
  • 4. Bring your hands apart. Drop the coin into your pocket.
  • 5. Open your empty hand, the coin has vanished!
